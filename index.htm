Quick Guide

  • Move around the map, this uses energy.
  • If you run out of energy, you die.
  • You can gather wood and stone from certain tiles on the map
  • You can build a campfire on an open map tile, this uses wood but provides energy.
  • Things you build are only active when your player is standing on them.
